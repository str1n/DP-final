  1. Non-compliance with the commitments linked to the loan: consequences for the Borrower

Interbridge Mortgages Limited expects you to:

  • Make the required payments on your mortgage on time

  • Not let the Property

  • Look after the Property and keep it in a good state of repair

  • Insure the Property using an appropriate policy

  • Pay any costs and charges associated with the Property, for example, any ground rent and service charges.

Where you fail to meet these (or other) obligations there may be additional costs for you where further work has to be carried out. Details of Interbridge Mortgages Limited’s fees and charges can be found in Interbridge Mortgages Limited’s Tariff of Fees and Charges and the Mortgage Conditions.

In addition, you will have to pay any costs Interbridge Mortgages Limited pay for insurance and to third parties (e.g. solicitors, asset managers, receivers) that they may instruct to recover any money owed to Interbridge Mortgages Limited or protect their security.

Interbridge Mortgages may also pay, on your behalf, any costs and charges associated with the Property where you have failed to and they reasonably consider it necessary to do so to protect their interest in the Property. Interbridge Mortgages Limited will add these costs and charges to the amount owed and that you have to repay to them.

Your full obligations are set out in the Mortgage Deed, the Mortgage Offer, including the Offer Conditions, the Mortgage Conditions and the Tariff of Fees and Charges which will all be provided to you.

If you fail to make your Monthly Payments due under the Mortgage, this information will be passed to credit reference agencies which may impact on your ability to take out further loans.

Should you encounter difficulties in making your Monthly Payments, please contact us straight away to explore possible solutions.

As a last resort, your home may be repossessed if you do not keep up with payments.
