Why is there additional interest on my statement?

Where additional interest is shown on your statement, this relates to interest on arrears and/or outstanding fees. Should you have arrears and/or fees balance, you will need to make additional payments in excess of your normal monthly instalment to clear these and prevent additional interest continuing to accrue. If you have not already done so, please contact us on 029 2254 0482 to make arrangements concerning this. The sooner you are able to pay these arrears and/or outstanding fees, the less interest that will accrue so we are keen to help you clear these off your account.

If you are having difficulties in making your monthly payments or if you have any queries in relation to your statement, please contact us on 029 2254 0482. We are here to help.

Thinking of paying less than your agreed monthly payment?

If you pay less than your agreed payment, in most cases it may cause you to be in arrears and cost you more to pay off your loan.

If you have diffculties making payments under your loan agreement, please contact us if you have not already done so to discuss how we can help. You may also want to seek advice on what to do from an independent free advice agency such as the Citizens Advice Bureau.

Want to overpay on your loan with us?

You can make Overpayments at any time. You should aways contact us before making any Overpayments so that we can provide guidance on how, considering your circumstances, the payment can be best applied to give you the most benefit.

Something not right on your statement?

If you have spotted something on your statement you are not sure about, or think might be incorrect, please give us a call and we will try to resolve your query.

We appreciate that sometimes we don't always get it right, but we would like the chance to put it right for you. Please call us on 029 2254 0482.
